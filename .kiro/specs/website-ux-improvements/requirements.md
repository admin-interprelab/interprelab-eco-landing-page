# Website User Experience Improvements - Requirements Document

## Introduction

This document outlines requirements for improving the user experience of the InterpreLab website to address the critical pain points faced by medical interpreters in today's industry. Based on comprehensive analysis of interpreter challenges including financial pressures, technological failures, psychological toll, and lack of professional development support, the focus is on creating an empathetic, supportive platform that acknowledges these struggles while providing hope and practical solutions through InterpreLab's AI-powered training and assistance tools.

## Glossary

- **InterpreLab_Platform**: The main website and ecosystem providing interpretation training and AI-powered tools designed to address industry pain points
- **Navigation_System**: The primary navigation menu and routing structure optimized for stressed and time-constrained interpreters
- **Content_Discovery**: The ability for users to find relevant solutions to their professional challenges and career development needs
- **User_Journey**: The carefully crafted path that takes interpreters from acknowledging their struggles to finding hope and practical solutions
- **Empathetic_Content**: Website messaging that validates interpreter experiences and addresses industry-specific challenges
- **Professional_Support**: Resources and tools designed to combat burnout, improve skills, and enhance career prospects
- **Accessibility_Standards**: WCAG compliance ensuring access for interpreters with varying abilities and stress levels

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a struggling interpreter facing industry challenges, I want to immediately feel understood and see that InterpreLab recognizes my professional pain points, so that I feel validated and motivated to explore solutions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user visits the homepage, THE Empathetic_Content SHALL acknowledge specific industry challenges like pay disparities, technological failures, and emotional toll within the first viewport
2. WHEN a user reads the value proposition, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL connect AI-powered solutions directly to interpreter pain points like cognitive overload and lack of professional development
3. WHILE a user explores initial content, THE Empathetic_Content SHALL use language that demonstrates deep understanding of interpreter experiences including burnout, vicarious trauma, and feeling undervalued
4. WHEN a user encounters problem statements, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL reference specific industry issues like the $0.10-$4.95 per minute pay disparity and technological compliance failures
5. WHERE users need immediate emotional connection, THE Empathetic_Content SHALL present interpreter voices and experiences that mirror their own struggles

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As an interpreter seeking hope and solutions, I want to discover how InterpreLab's tools specifically address my daily challenges, so that I can see a path forward in my career.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user explores tool descriptions, THE Professional_Support SHALL clearly connect each AI tool to specific pain point solutions (InterpreCoach for cognitive load, InterpreStudy for accessible training)
2. WHEN a user views feature explanations, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL demonstrate how AI assistance reduces stress factors like terminology lookup, note-taking, and real-time support
3. WHILE a user navigates tool categories, THE Content_Discovery SHALL organize solutions by interpreter needs: skill development, stress reduction, career advancement, and emotional support
4. WHEN a user seeks professional development options, THE Professional_Support SHALL highlight how AI-powered training addresses the $1000+ cost barrier and lack of specialized courses
5. WHERE users need reassurance about technology, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL contrast reliable AI assistance with the unstable platforms that currently frustrate interpreters

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a time-constrained interpreter, I want fast, intuitive navigation that respects my limited time and high stress levels, so that I can quickly find relevant information without additional frustration.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user navigates between pages, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL complete page transitions within 2 seconds to avoid adding technological stress
2. WHEN a user loads any page, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL display meaningful content within 1.5 seconds recognizing interpreters' time constraints
3. WHILE pages are loading, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL show calming, professional loading indicators that reduce anxiety
4. WHEN a user experiences slow connections, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL gracefully degrade while maintaining access to critical information about solutions
5. IF a page fails to load, THEN THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL provide supportive error messages with immediate alternatives, avoiding additional stress

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As an interpreter who may be experiencing stress-related accessibility needs, I want the website to be fully accessible and considerate of my mental state, so that I can access information regardless of my current abilities or stress level.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user navigates with keyboard only, THE Navigation_System SHALL provide clear focus indicators and logical tab order suitable for users with tremors or motor difficulties from stress
2. WHEN a user uses screen readers, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL provide appropriate ARIA labels that clearly describe emotional support and professional development content
3. WHILE viewing content, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL maintain sufficient color contrast and calming color schemes that support users experiencing anxiety or depression
4. WHEN a user encounters interactive elements, THE Navigation_System SHALL provide clear feedback without overwhelming stressed users with excessive animations or sounds
5. WHERE multimedia content exists, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL provide alternative text and captions recognizing that some users may have audio processing difficulties due to occupational stress

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As an interpreter on mobile devices between assignments, I want seamless mobile access to supportive content and tools, so that I can find encouragement and resources during breaks or commutes.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user accesses the site on mobile devices, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL display responsive layouts optimized for quick consumption of supportive content
2. WHEN a user interacts with touch elements, THE Navigation_System SHALL provide appropriate touch targets of at least 44px, considering that stress can affect fine motor control
3. WHILE using mobile navigation, THE Navigation_System SHALL prioritize access to immediate support resources and quick tool previews
4. WHEN a user views content on mobile, THE Empathetic_Content SHALL maintain readability and emotional impact without requiring horizontal scrolling
5. WHERE mobile-specific features are available, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL optimize for quick access to stress-relief resources and professional development tools

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** As an interpreter gradually building hope through the platform, I want to see a clear progression from problem acknowledgment to solution discovery to taking action, so that I feel guided toward positive change.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user progresses through the site, THE User_Journey SHALL follow a therapeutic progression from validation to hope-building to empowerment
2. WHEN a user moves from problem awareness to solution exploration, THE Content_Discovery SHALL provide smooth transitions that maintain emotional engagement
3. WHILE a user explores different sections, THE Professional_Support SHALL progressively reveal success stories and evidence of positive outcomes
4. WHEN a user reaches decision points, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL provide clear next steps that feel achievable rather than overwhelming
5. WHERE users need encouragement to continue, THE Empathetic_Content SHALL provide motivational messaging that acknowledges their courage in seeking change

### Requirement 7

**User Story:** As an interpreter ready to invest in my professional future, I want transparent, compelling premium offerings that feel like an investment in my wellbeing rather than another financial burden, so that I can make confident decisions about upgrading.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user has been emotionally engaged through the journey, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL present premium offerings as investments in professional wellbeing and career advancement
2. WHEN a user views pricing information, THE Content_Discovery SHALL frame costs in terms of potential earnings improvement and stress reduction value
3. WHILE a user evaluates premium features, THE Professional_Support SHALL demonstrate clear ROI through reduced cognitive load, improved accuracy, and career advancement opportunities
4. WHEN a user compares plans, THE InterpreLab_Platform SHALL highlight features that directly address the pain points they've acknowledged (specialized training, real-time support, stress management)
5. WHERE users need final reassurance, THE Content_Discovery SHALL provide testimonials from interpreters who transformed their careers and wellbeing through the platform
